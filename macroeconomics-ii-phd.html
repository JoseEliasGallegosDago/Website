Macroeconomics II (PhD)

IIES, Stockholm University — Spring 2020

I TA this course for Sergio de Ferra (the first part, Real Business Cycles and the New Keynesian model, is taught by John Hassler and TA'd by Gualtiero Azzalini). In the assignments we cover open-economy macro models, international capital flows, exchange rates and nominal rigidities, financial frictions and optimal policy. Please send your solutions to my email: jose.elias.gallegos@bde.es.
